Facebook Reviews plugin allow you get rating from facebook page to your Wordpress site.

== Description ==
Facebook Reviews plugin allow you get rating from facebook page to your Wordpress site. People do give reviews regarding the company/website/services on the Facebook page. If you receive any testimonials on your Facebook page, you should share it on your store too. Showcasing testimonials and reviews of the Facebook page on your store allows you to increase your brand credibility and leads to faster sales.
* Free!
* Show Facebook Reviews on website to increase user confidence, traffic and sales
* Trim long reviews so that your users can read them comfortably
* Your customers are already viewing your site so why not make it easy for them to view your positive reviews? Now you can! With Facebook Reviews you can quickly and easily embed your reviews directly on your store.

== Installation ==
1. Unpack archive to this archive to the \'wp-content/plugins/\' directory inside of WordPress
2. Activate the plugin through the \'Plugins\' menu in WordPress
3. Add this shortcode [otfbrv {parameters}] to your post or page content or using widget

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How can I find my page ID in Facebook? =
To find your page id in facebook::
1. Go to your page
2. Click "About"
3. Click "Page Infor"
4. You can see "Facebook Page ID"

= How can I get Page access token? =
1. Go to <a href="https://developers.facebook.com/" target="_blank">developers.facebook.com</a> to Log in using your personal Facebook credentials or Register if this is your first time signing in to the Facebook Developer portal.
2. Hover over My Apps and then click on Add a New App.
3. When your App is now set up.  Click on Dashboard to find your app information.
4. Go to <a href="https://developers.facebook.com/tools/accesstoken/">Access Token Tool of Facebook</a> to get Access Token.
